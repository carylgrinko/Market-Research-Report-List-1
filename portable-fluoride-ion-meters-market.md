<p><p>The portable fluoride ion meters market comprises three main types: ion electrodes, reference electrodes, and temperature electrodes. Ion electrodes are specifically designed to measure the concentration of fluoride ions in various samples. Reference electrodes provide a stable voltage against which the ion electrode's readings can be compared, ensuring accuracy. Temperature electrodes monitor the sample temperature and adjust readings accordingly, as temperature can affect ion concentration measurements. Together, these components contribute to precise fluoride analysis in diverse applications.</p></p>
