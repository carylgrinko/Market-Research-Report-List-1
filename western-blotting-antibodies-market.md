<p><p>The Western Blotting Antibodies market comprises two main types: monoclonal and polyclonal antibodies. Monoclonal antibodies are derived from a single B-cell clone, ensuring specificity and uniformity in detecting target proteins, making them ideal for consistent experimental results. In contrast, polyclonal antibodies are generated from multiple B-cell lineages, providing a broader recognition of proteins but with possible variations in specificity. Both types play crucial roles in research, diagnostics, and clinical applications, catering to diverse scientific needs.</p></p>
