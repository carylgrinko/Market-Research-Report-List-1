<p><p>The PM2.5 air quality monitoring system market is categorized into several types, including TEOM (Tapered Element Oscillating Microbalance) Monitors, Beta Attenuation Monitors, and others. TEOM monitors measure particulate matter by detecting changes in frequency, providing real-time data on PM2.5 levels. Beta Attenuation Monitors assess air quality by measuring the decrease in beta radiation as it passes through collected particles. Other types may include optical monitors and gravimetric methods, each offering varying technical capabilities and applications for effective air quality management.</p></p>
