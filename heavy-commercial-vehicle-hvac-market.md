<p><ul><li>Engine Powered HVAC</li><li>Electric Powered HVAC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Heavy Commercial Vehicle HVAC market comprises two primary types: Engine Powered HVAC and Electric Powered HVAC. Engine Powered HVAC systems rely on the vehicle's internal combustion engine for operation, utilizing engine-driven compressors to provide heating and cooling. In contrast, Electric Powered HVAC systems operate using electric power, often sourced from batteries or alternators, offering advantages such as reduced emissions and quieter operation. Both systems cater to different vehicle types and operational needs, affecting efficiency and environmental impact.</p></p>
