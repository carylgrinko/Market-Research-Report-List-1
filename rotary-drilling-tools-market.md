<p><strong>The Rotary Drilling Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Augers</li><li>Drilling Buckets</li><li>Core Barrels</li><li>Temporary Casing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary drilling tools market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Augers** are spiral-shaped tools used for boring holes in soil or rock, ideal for shallow applications.</p><p>2. **Drilling Buckets** are cylindrical containers that collect soil and debris during drilling, suitable for larger, deeper excavations.</p><p>3. **Core Barrels** retrieve cylindrical samples of rock or soil, crucial for geological analysis.</p><p>4. **Temporary Casing** protects the borehole during drilling and supports the walls, ensuring stability and preventing collapse. Each type serves distinct functions in various drilling applications.</p></p>
