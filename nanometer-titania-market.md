<p><p>The nanometer titania market is categorized mainly into two types: rutile and anatase. Rutile, characterized by its high refractive index and superior UV resistance, is widely used in applications like coatings and plastics. Anatase, on the other hand, is known for its photocatalytic properties, making it ideal for environmental applications like air and water purification. Both forms are vital in various industries, including electronics, cosmetics, and construction, due to their unique characteristics and functionalities.</p></p>
