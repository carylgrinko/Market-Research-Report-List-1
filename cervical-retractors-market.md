<p><strong>The Cervical Retractors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transverse Retractors</li><li>Longitudinal Retractors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cervical retractors market is divided into two main types: transverse retractors and longitudinal retractors. Transverse retractors are designed to hold back tissues laterally during cervical surgeries, providing better visibility and access to the surgical site. In contrast, longitudinal retractors are utilized to manage tissue vertically, facilitating a clearer view of deeper structures. Both types are essential for enhancing surgical precision and minimizing tissue trauma, thereby improving patient outcomes during cervical procedures.</p></p>
