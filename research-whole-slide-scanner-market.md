<p><p>The research whole slide scanner market encompasses various types, primarily brightfield and fluorescence scanning technologies. Brightfield scanners illuminate samples with white light, producing images based on transmitted light, ideal for traditional histology and pathology applications. In contrast, fluorescence scanners utilize specific wavelengths to excite fluorochromes within the samples, enabling the visualization of cellular structures and processes with high sensitivity. Both technologies play vital roles in diagnostics, research, and education, providing detailed insights into biological specimens and facilitating advanced analysis.</p></p>
