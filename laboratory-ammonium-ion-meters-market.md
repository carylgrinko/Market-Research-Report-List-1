<p><p>Laboratory ammonium ion meters are essential instruments for measuring ammonium ion concentration in various samples. The market can be divided into two primary types: single input and dual input meters. Single input meters measure ammonium ions from one sample at a time, making them suitable for basic applications. In contrast, dual input meters allow simultaneous measurement from two samples, enhancing efficiency and productivity for laboratories handling multiple analyses. Both types cater to specific research needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
