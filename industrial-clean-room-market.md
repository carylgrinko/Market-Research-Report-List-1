<p><strong>The Industrial Clean Room Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Turbulently Ventilated</li><li>Unidirectional Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial clean room market is categorized into two main types: turbulently ventilated and unidirectional flow clean rooms. Turbulently ventilated clean rooms use a more conventional air circulation method, where air is mixed throughout the space, allowing for some variability in particle levels. In contrast, unidirectional flow clean rooms feature laminar airflow, directing clean air in a straight line, minimizing contamination risks. Both designs cater to specific industry needs, emphasizing cleanliness and the control of airborne particles for optimal operational efficiency.</p></p>
