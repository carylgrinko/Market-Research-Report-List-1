The market is divided into two key types: whole body plethysmography and part-of-body plethysmography. Whole body plethysmography involves encasing the entire body in a sealed chamber to gauge total lung capacity and other respiratory metrics, while part-of-body plethysmography focuses on specific regions, such as the chest, to analyze localized lung functions or conditions.</p></p>
