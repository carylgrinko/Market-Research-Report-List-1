<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electronic Cartography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Chart Systems (ECS)</li><li>Raster Chart Display Systems (RCDS)</li><li>Electronic Chart Display and Information Systems (ECDIS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic cartography market encompasses various systems designed for navigating and monitoring maritime activities. Electronic Chart Systems (ECS) provide navigational data but lack the mandatory compliance of ECDIS. Raster Chart Display Systems (RCDS) utilize scanned images of paper nautical charts, offering a familiar interface for mariners. In contrast, Electronic Chart Display and Information Systems (ECDIS) integrate electronic nautical charts with real-time navigational data, ensuring compliance with international maritime regulations, enhancing safety and efficiency in navigation.</p></p>
