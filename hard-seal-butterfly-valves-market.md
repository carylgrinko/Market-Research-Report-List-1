<p><p>The Hard Seal Butterfly Valves market consists of two main types: flanged and flangeless. Flanged butterfly valves feature protruding flanges for easy attachment to piping systems, ensuring robust connections and facilitating maintenance. In contrast, flangeless butterfly valves are designed for direct mounting, providing a more streamlined installation process and saving space in tight environments. Both types are used for regulating fluid flow in various applications, with the choice between them depending on specific installation requirements and engineering preferences.</p></p>
