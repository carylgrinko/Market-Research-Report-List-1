<p><p>The oral cancer therapeutic market consists of various treatment types, primarily targeted therapy and chemotherapy. Targeted therapy focuses on specific genetic traits and molecular markers of cancer cells, aiming to inhibit their growth with minimal effects on healthy tissues. Conversely, chemotherapy involves the use of cytotoxic drugs to kill rapidly dividing cells, affecting both cancerous and healthy tissue, often leading to significant side effects. Both treatment approaches are essential in managing oral cancer and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
