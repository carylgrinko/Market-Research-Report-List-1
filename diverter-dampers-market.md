<p><p>Diverter dampers play a crucial role in directing airflow in industrial applications. The market comprises three primary types: Manual, Electric, and Pneumatic. Manual diverter dampers require human operation to control airflow, offering simplicity and low cost. Electric types utilize motors for precise, automated control, suitable for complex systems. Pneumatic diverter dampers rely on compressed air for actuation, providing quick response times and efficiency. Each type serves distinct industry needs based on functionality, operating environment, and budgetary constraints.</p></p>
