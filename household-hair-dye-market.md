<p><p>The household hair dye market consists of three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes offer a short-term change, typically lasting until the next wash, ideal for festivals or events. Semi-permanent dyes provide longer-lasting color, usually fading over several weeks and gradually washing out without harsh chemicals. Permanent hair dyes penetrate the hair shaft for long-lasting color that can endure until new growth appears, making them popular for covering gray hair or achieving a dramatic change.</p></p>
