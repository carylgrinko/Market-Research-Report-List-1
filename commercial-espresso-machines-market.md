<p><p>The commercial espresso machine market comprises three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require baristas to exert complete control over the brewing process, offering a traditional experience. Semi-automatic machines combine manual grinding and tamping with automated water flow, striking a balance between skill and convenience. Fully automatic machines simplify the process by automating most functions, allowing for consistent output with minimal effort. Each type caters to different user preferences and business needs in the coffee industry.</p></p>
