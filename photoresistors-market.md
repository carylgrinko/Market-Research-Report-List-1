<p><p>The photoresistor market encompasses various types based on the sensitivity to different light wavelengths. UV Light Dependent Resistors (ULDRs) are designed to respond to ultraviolet light, making them ideal for applications requiring UV detection. Infrared Photosensitive Resistors are tailored for infrared light, commonly used in remote sensing and thermal applications. Visible Light Dependent Resistors are sensitive to the visible spectrum, widely utilized in ambient light sensing. Additionally, the "Other" market includes specialized resistors that cater to unconventional light detection needs.</p></p>
