<p><p>Fire hose testers are essential tools used to evaluate the integrity and performance of fire hoses under pressure. The market is primarily divided into two types: electric and pneumatic testers. Electric testers utilize electrically powered pumps to create the required pressure, offering precise control and efficiency. In contrast, pneumatic testers use compressed air to achieve pressure, often providing portability and ease of use. Both types cater to various operational needs, ensuring fire hoses meet safety and performance standards.</p></p>
