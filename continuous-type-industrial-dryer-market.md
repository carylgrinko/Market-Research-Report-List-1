<p><p>The continuous type industrial dryer market includes various technologies designed for efficient drying of materials. Tunnel dryers use a conveyor system to move products through a heated chamber, ensuring uniform drying. Disc dryers employ heated discs to transfer heat directly to the material, suitable for viscous substances. Paddle dryers utilize rotating paddles to mix and expose materials to heat, optimizing moisture removal. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the material and production needs, enhancing operational efficiency in industrial processes.</p></p>
