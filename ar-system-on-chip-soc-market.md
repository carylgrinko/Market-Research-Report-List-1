<p><p>The Augmented Reality (AR) System on Chip (SoC) market comprises two main types: standalone and tethered solutions. Standalone AR SoCs are integrated devices that operate independently, providing all necessary processing, graphics, and connectivity without needing external hardware. Tethered AR SoCs, on the other hand, rely on external devices, such as smartphones or PCs, for processing power, offering enhanced capabilities but requiring a physical connection. Each type serves different applications and user preferences within the AR ecosystem.</p></p>
