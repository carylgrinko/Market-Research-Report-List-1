<p><p>The Airborne Surface Disinfection market primarily includes two types: Vaporized and Fogging. Vaporized disinfection involves using a vaporization mechanism to disperse disinfectant in a fine mist, effectively targeting surfaces and air in enclosed spaces. Fogging, on the other hand, uses a fog generator to produce larger droplets of disinfectant, allowing for broader coverage over surfaces. Both methods aim to eliminate pathogens and create a safer environment, catering to various settings such as healthcare, hospitality, and public spaces.</p></p>
