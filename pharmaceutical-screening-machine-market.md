<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Screening Machine</li><li>Vibration Screening Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical screening machine market includes two primary types: electromagnetic screening machines and vibration screening machines. Electromagnetic screening machines utilize magnetic fields to separate and analyze particles based on their properties, enhancing precision in pharmaceutical applications. In contrast, vibration screening machines employ mechanical vibrations to facilitate the classification and separation of materials, ensuring uniformity and quality control in the production process. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing drug formulation and ensuring compliance with industry standards.</p></p>
