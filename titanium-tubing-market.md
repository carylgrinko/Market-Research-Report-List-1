<p><ul><li>Straight Tubes</li><li>U-Bent Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The titanium tubing market comprises primarily two types: straight tubes and U-bent tubes. Straight tubes are characterized by their uniformity and versatility, suitable for various industrial applications, including aerospace and biomedical sectors. In contrast, U-bent tubes have a distinct curvature that allows for more complex designs, often used in heat exchangers and exhaust systems. Both types leverage titanium's lightweight, corrosion-resistant properties, making them ideal for high-performance applications while enhancing durability and efficiency in demanding environments.</p></p>
