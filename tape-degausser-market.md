<p><p>Tape degaussers come in three primary types: coil degaussers, which utilize electromagnetic coils to eliminate magnetic fields from tapes; capacitive discharge degaussers, which release a burst of stored energy to disrupt magnetic patterns; and permanent magnet degaussers, which employ strong permanent magnets to demagnetize tapes. Each type caters to different requirements in data sanitization, offering varying levels of effectiveness, speed, and portability, thus serving diverse industries with specific data destruction needs.</p></p>
