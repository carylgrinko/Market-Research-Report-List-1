<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber Wraps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rayon Based</li><li>Pitch Based</li><li>Pan Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon fiber wraps market can be categorized into three primary types based on the precursor materials used: Rayon-based, Pitch-based, and PAN (Polyacrylonitrile) based carbon fibers. </p><p>Rayon-based fibers offer a cost-effective option with lower tensile strength, commonly used in textiles. Pitch-based fibers provide high thermal conductivity and stiffness, suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. PAN-based fibers, the most prevalent, deliver superior tensile strength and lightweight properties, making them ideal for high-performance composites in various industries, including sports equipment and industrial applications.</p></p>
