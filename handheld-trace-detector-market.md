<p><ul><li>Mass Spectroscopy</li><li>Ion Mobility Spectrometry</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Handheld Trace Detector market is primarily categorized into three types: Mass Spectrometry, Ion Mobility Spectrometry, and Others. Mass Spectrometry involves analyzing the mass-to-charge ratio of ions, providing detailed information about chemical compositions. Ion Mobility Spectrometry measures the drift time of ions through a gas, allowing for rapid detection of trace substances. The "Others" category includes alternative detection technologies such as thermal desorption and fluorescence, which diversify the capabilities and applications of handheld detection devices in various fields.</p></p>
