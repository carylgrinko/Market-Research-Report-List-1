<p><strong>The Marine SOx Scrubber System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Closed Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Hybrid Scrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine SOx Scrubber System market comprises three main types: open loop scrubbers, closed loop scrubbers, and hybrid scrubbers. Open loop scrubbers use seawater to remove sulfur oxides from exhaust gases, discharging the cleaned water back into the ocean. Closed loop scrubbers, on the other hand, use freshwater mixed with alkali substances to neutralize SOx emissions, recycling the water within the system. Hybrid scrubbers can operate in both modes, providing flexibility based on operational conditions and environmental regulations.</p></p>
