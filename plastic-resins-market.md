<p><p>The plastic resins market consists of several key types, each with distinct properties and applications. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) is known for its strength and chemical resistance, making it ideal for containers and pipes. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is flexible and used in films and bags. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is commonly used in bottles and food packaging due to its durability. Polypropylene (PP) is valued for its versatility in automotive and consumer products. Polystyrene is often used for insulation and packaging. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) is widely used in construction and plumbing. Other resins include engineering plastics and specialty polymers, catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
