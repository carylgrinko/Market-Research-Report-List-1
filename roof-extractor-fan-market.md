<p><p>The roof extractor fan market consists of several types, including centrifugal, axial, and others. Centrifugal fans use a rotating impeller to draw air through the fan, efficiently expelling it at a right angle, ideal for high-pressure applications. Axial fans move air along the axis of the fan, making them suitable for low-pressure, high-volume ventilation. Other types may include mixed-flow or inline fans, each tailored for specific ventilation needs and settings, catering to diverse industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
