<p><p>The therapeutic enzyme drugs market comprises three primary types based on administration routes: injectable, oral, and topical. Injectable enzymes are delivered via syringes or infusions, ensuring rapid systemic action for conditions like enzyme deficiencies. Oral enzymes are consumed in pill or liquid forms, offering convenience for chronic conditions but may have slower absorption rates. Topical enzymes are applied directly to the skin, targeting localized conditions such as wounds or skin disorders, promoting healing and reducing inflammation effectively.</p></p>
