<p><ul><li>2 Pipes System</li><li>4 Pipes System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-function heat pumps can be categorized into two main systems: the 2-pipe system and the 4-pipe system. The 2-pipe system operates by using a single set of pipes for both heating and cooling, switching the flow based on the desired function. In contrast, the 4-pipe system features separate pipes for heating and cooling, allowing simultaneous operation of both functions. This differentiation offers versatility in temperature control and efficiency, catering to varying climate demands in residential and commercial sectors.</p></p>
