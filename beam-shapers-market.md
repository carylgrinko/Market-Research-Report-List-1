<p><ul><li>Top-Hat Beam Shapers</li><li>Flat-Top Beam Shapers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Beam shapers are optical devices used to transform laser beams into specific spatial profiles for various applications. Top-hat beam shapers create a uniform intensity distribution across a flat, rectangular area, making them ideal for processes requiring consistent illumination. Flat-top beam shapers, on the other hand, produce a similarly uniform profile but typically utilize cylindrical or elliptical shapes tailored for specific tasks. Both types enhance precision in industrial, medical, and research settings, optimizing energy delivery and improving performance outcomes.</p></p>
