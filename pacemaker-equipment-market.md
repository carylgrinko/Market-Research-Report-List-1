<p><strong>The Pacemaker Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Leadless Pacemaker</li><li>Single Chamber Pacemaker</li><li>Dual Chamber Pacemaker</li><li>Biventricular Pacemaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pacemaker equipment market includes various types catering to different cardiac needs. A leadless pacemaker is a compact device directly implanted in the heart, minimizing infection risks. A single chamber pacemaker regulates one heart chamber, mainly used for bradycardia. A dual chamber pacemaker controls two chambers, enhancing coordination between them. Biventricular pacemakers address heart failure by stimulating both ventricles for improved efficiency. Each type serves specific patient requirements, reflecting advancements in cardiac care technology.</p></p>
