<p><p>The CNC rotary surface grinder market can be categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on the orientation of the grinding spindle. Vertical grinders feature a vertically oriented spindle, allowing for efficient grinding of complex workpieces and better chip removal, making them ideal for small, heavy components. In contrast, horizontal grinders have a horizontally positioned spindle, suitable for larger workpieces and enabling high precision over extended surfaces. Both types cater to distinct industrial needs, influencing their market demand and application.</p></p>
