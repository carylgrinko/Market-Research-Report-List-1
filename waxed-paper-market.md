<p><p>The waxed paper market is primarily divided into two types: natural-based wax paper and mineral-based wax paper. Natural-based wax paper is made using organic materials, such as beeswax or plant-based waxes, providing eco-friendly options ideal for food packaging and wrapping. In contrast, mineral-based wax paper incorporates paraffin wax, derived from petroleum, offering enhanced durability and moisture resistance, making it suitable for industrial applications. Both types serve distinct purposes, catering to varying consumer preferences and market demands.</p></p>
