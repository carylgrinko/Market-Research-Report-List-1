<p><p>The magnesium and magnesium alloy market is primarily segmented into cast alloys and wrought alloys. Cast alloys are created by pouring molten magnesium into molds, offering complex shapes and high strength-to-weight ratios, commonly used in automotive and aerospace applications. In contrast, wrought alloys are processed through mechanical or thermal methods, enhancing their structural integrity and ductility, making them suitable for applications in machinery and construction. Both types play crucial roles in industries seeking lightweight yet durable materials.</p></p>
