<p><p>The microplate luminometer market consists of two primary types: single-mode microplate luminometers and multimode microplate luminometers. Single-mode luminometers are specialized instruments designed exclusively for measuring luminescence in biological assays, offering high sensitivity for specific applications. In contrast, multimode luminometers provide versatility by accommodating multiple detection methods, including fluorescence, absorbance, and luminescence, making them suitable for a wider range of experiments. This flexibility serves various sectors, such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and environmental monitoring, enhancing research capabilities.</p></p>
