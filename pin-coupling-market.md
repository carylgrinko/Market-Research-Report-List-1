<p><p>Pin coupling is a mechanical device used to connect two shafts, allowing them to transmit torque while accommodating misalignment. It consists of pins that engage with holes in the couplings at the ends of the shafts, providing a reliable connection that can handle varying levels of stress. Pin couplings are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing due to their ability to transmit high torque and withstand dynamic loads.</p><p>The Pin Coupling Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.9% during the forecast period.
