<p><ul><li>Metal Fixing Plate</li><li>Plastic Fixing Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Arthrodesis plates, used in surgical procedures to fuse joints, are primarily available in two types: metal fixing plates and plastic fixing plates. Metal fixing plates, typically made from titanium or stainless steel, offer strength and durability, making them suitable for high-load-bearing applications. In contrast, plastic fixing plates, often made from biocompatible polymers, provide flexibility and are used in specific cases where lower rigidity is desired. Both types cater to various clinical needs, influencing the choice based on patient conditions and surgical preferences.</p></p>
