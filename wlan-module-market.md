<p><strong>The WLAN Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded WLAN Modules</li><li>External WLAN Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The WLAN module market is divided into two main types: embedded WLAN modules and external WLAN modules. Embedded WLAN modules are integrated directly into devices, providing seamless connectivity while saving space and reducing assembly complexity. They are commonly found in smartphones, tablets, and IoT devices. In contrast, external WLAN modules are standalone units, often connected via USB or other interfaces, offering flexibility for devices that lack built-in Wi-Fi capability. They are popular in PC peripherals and legacy equipment requiring wireless connectivity.</p></p>
