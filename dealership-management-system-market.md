<p><p>The Dealership Management System (DMS) market can be categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based DMS offers flexibility and accessibility, allowing dealerships to access their systems from anywhere with an internet connection. This model often includes continuous updates and lower initial costs. In contrast, on-premises systems are installed locally on dealership servers, providing greater control over data and security. However, they come with higher upfront costs and require ongoing maintenance and IT support.</p></p>
