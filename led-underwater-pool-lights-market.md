<p><strong>The LED Underwater Pool Lights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface-mounted</li><li>Flush-mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED underwater pool lights market is categorized into surface-mounted and flush-mounted types. Surface-mounted lights are installed on the pool's wall, protruding slightly above the surface, making them easier to maintain and replace. Conversely, flush-mounted lights are embedded within the pool structure, providing a sleek, seamless look. Both types enhance pool aesthetics and safety, offering energy-efficient lighting solutions. The choice between them often depends on design preferences, installation considerations, and the desired lighting effect in the pool environment.</p></p>
