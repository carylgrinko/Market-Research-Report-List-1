<p><p>Smart ticketing encompasses various market types designed to enhance convenience and efficiency in fare collection. E-kiosks combine the functions of ticket machines with advanced features like touchscreen interfaces and real-time updates, providing users with interactive support for purchasing and managing travel tickets seamlessly. Together, these solutions streamline public transportation access.</p></p>
