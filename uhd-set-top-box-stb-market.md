<p><p>The UHD Set-Top Box (STB) market encompasses various segments including Cable, Satellite, DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television), IP (Internet Protocol), and OTT (Over-The-Top) platforms. Cable STBs deliver high-definition content via traditional cable networks, while Satellite STBs receive signals from satellite dishes. DTT STBs access digital broadcasts over the air. IP STBs stream content through internet connections, and OTT STBs facilitate access to streaming services without traditional subscription models. Each type caters to different viewing preferences and technological infrastructures.</p></p>
