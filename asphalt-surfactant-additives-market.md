<p><ul><li>Cationic Additive</li><li>Anion Additive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The asphalt surfactant additives market is primarily divided into two types: cationic and anionic additives. Cationic additives carry a positive charge, enhancing the adhesion of asphalt to aggregates and improving overall durability. They are especially useful in colder climates where moisture can weaken asphalt mixtures. Anionic additives, carrying a negative charge, help in stabilizing emulsions and enhancing water resistance, making them suitable for various applications. Both types optimize the performance and longevity of asphalt in construction and paving projects.</p></p>
