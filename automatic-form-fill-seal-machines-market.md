<p><ul><li>Horizontal Form Fill Seal Machines</li><li>Vertical Form Fill Seal Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Form Fill Seal Machines market is divided into two primary types: Horizontal Form Fill Seal (HFFS) machines and Vertical Form Fill Seal (VFFS) machines. HFFS machines operate by forming pouches from a flat film, filling them horizontally, making them ideal for products requiring a stable fill. In contrast, VFFS machines create bags from a roll of film vertically, suitable for packaging granules and liquids, offering versatility and efficient space utilization. Both types enhance packaging speed and consistency.</p></p>
