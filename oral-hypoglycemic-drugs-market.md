<p><p>The oral hypoglycemic drugs market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Sulfonylureas (SU)** stimulate insulin secretion from the pancreas, effectively lowering blood glucose levels.</p><p>2. **Biguanides**, with Metformin as a leading example, enhance insulin sensitivity and decrease hepatic glucose production.</p><p>3. **Alpha-glucosidase inhibitors** slow carbohydrate digestion and absorption, helping to manage postprandial blood sugar spikes.</p><p>4. **Insulin synergists** enhance the effects of insulin, improving glucose uptake in tissues. Each class plays a vital role in diabetes management.</p></p>
