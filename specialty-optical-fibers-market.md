<p><p>The Specialty Optical Fibers market includes Multi-Mode and Single-Mode fibers, each designed for specific applications. Multi-Mode Specialty Optical Fiber supports multiple light paths, making it ideal for short-distance, high-bandwidth communication, such as in data centers and local area networks. Single-Mode Specialty Optical Fiber allows only one light path, enabling long-distance, high-speed data transmission with minimal signal loss, commonly used in telecommunications and long-haul networks. Both types are tailored for advanced performance in specialized environments, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
