<p><p>The heavy calcium carbonate market is segmented into dry and wet processes. The dry process involves crushing and grinding limestone to produce fine particles, making it suitable for applications like plastics and coatings. In contrast, the wet process uses water to enhance the quality and purity of the calcium carbonate, resulting in finer particle sizes, often used in pharmaceuticals and food industries. Both methods cater to specific industrial needs, influencing product characteristics and market demand.</p></p>
