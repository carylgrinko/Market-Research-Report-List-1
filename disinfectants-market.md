<p><strong>The Disinfectants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidising Disinfectants</li><li>Non-oxidising Disinfectants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disinfectants market is mainly divided into two types: oxidizing and non-oxidizing disinfectants. Oxidizing disinfectants, such as chlorine and hydrogen peroxide, work by releasing reactive oxygen species that destroy microbial cells, making them effective against a wide range of pathogens. Non-oxidizing disinfectants, including quaternary ammonium compounds and phenolics, act by disrupting microbial cell membranes or inhibiting metabolic processes. Both types are essential in various applications, including healthcare, food processing, and sanitation, ensuring effective infection control and hygiene maintenance.</p></p>
