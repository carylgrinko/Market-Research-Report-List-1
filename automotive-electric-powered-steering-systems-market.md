<p><p>The automotive electric power steering (EPS) systems market includes various types tailored for different vehicle designs. The column type features an electric motor integrated into the steering column, providing compactness and efficiency. The pinion-and-rack type uses an electric motor to assist in steering through a pinion gear, offering precise control. The double pinion type enhances steering responsiveness with dual motor assistance. The ball screw type employs a ball screw mechanism for smooth steering, combining efficiency with reduced friction, ideal for luxury vehicles.</p></p>
